Projects that wish to adopt Ofun principles and be included via this page have a few options. They can add the text Ofunin2013 to one (only one please!) of the pages for their project so they appear in this google search for Ofunin2013. They can (instead or additionally) edit this page (and ideally add their own page to this site). Or they can do nothing and feel sad.
  • Pugs, which gave birth to -Ofun, is an implementation of Perl 6 written in Haskell. About Pugs and Ofun.
  • Perl 6, which gave birth to Pugs, is a new dialect of Perl. About Perl 6 and Ofun.
  • perl-compiler is an implementation of Perl 5 that enables flexible code-generation (unlike standard perl). About perl-compiler and Ofun.
  • Perlito, which is a child of Pugs, is an implementation of Perl 5 & 6 subsets. About Perlito and Ofun.
  • YAPC::NA 2013 is an upcoming North America Perl conference. About YAPC::NA 2013 and Ofun.
