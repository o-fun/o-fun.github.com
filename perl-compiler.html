perl-compiler and Ofun

The perl-compiler enables hackable and static Perl 5 optimizations, in contrast to standard stable perl. Esp. B::CC.

This project is tentatively participating as an Ofun4Compiler-O project.

  • Examples of instant gratification: perlcc -O -O3 -fno-autovivify, -fno-magic
    • Up to 6x faster perl in the Benchmark Game
  • Items to invoke a sense of wonder:
    • perl5 TODOs
    • Big module sizes
  • Discovery links:
    • perl-compiler.org
