Perl 6 and Ofun

Perl 6, which gave birth to Pugs, is a dialect of Perl.

As the Perl 6 website says, "Perl 6 is optimized for fun, short -Ofun. Perl 6 has a fun-driven community: writing a Perl 6 compiler is fun, developing applications too, and most of all dealing with friendly, intelligent people."

The overall Perl 6 project and culture is an Ofun4FLOSS project. Individual compilers may or may not be Ofun4Compiler projects. Pugs, the original -Ofun project, definitely is. Rakudo both is (due to it following many of the Ofun aspects of its Perl 6 context) and is not (mostly because it requires signing of an agreement to gain a commit bit).
